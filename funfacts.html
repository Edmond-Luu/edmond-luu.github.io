Fun Facts

In this page, you'll learn about some interesting and fun facts about me that I did not share in the about section.

  • When I was a little kid, I really enjoyed watching and performing magic tricks which made me dream of becoming a magician when I grow up.
  • I built a large city on Minecraft which featured a beautiful international airport. Unfortunately, my hard drive failed one day and I thought I backed up that file but in fact I backed up a much older version of that file from a while ago. I lost years of hard work because of that. :(
  • I enjoy learning languages; I have learned Korean on my own and can type about 60 WPM on a Korean keyboard.
  • I am proficient in reading and writing Simplified and Traditional Chinese even though I never formally studied or attended classes. I naturally learned it by listening to Chinese news and music while reading the subtitles as well as practicing on my own. It's safe to say that I am the only person in my family of my generation who can read and write Chinese.
  • I enjoy listening to older songs much more than newer songs. I especially love sentimental ballads. Some of my favorite songs are Making Love Out of Nothing At All (Air Supply), Rhythm of the Rain (The Cascades), and How Deep is Your Love (Bee Gees).
  • I prefer using metric units rather than US customary or imperial units. The weather app on my phone is set to celcius for temperature, kilometers per hour for wind, and millibars for pressure.
  • I love geography; I can name a lot of countries on a world map and when I was in 6th grade, I could draw a pretty accurate map of Asia off the top of my head.
  • In my free time, I like listening to music, watching YouTube videos, and reading about politics and history.
